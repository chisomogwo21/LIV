My Story
Olivia Abdalla is a filmmaker and writer dedicated to uncovering the intersection of identity, culture, and the human condition. With a background in film production and narrative direction, her work explores the unseen rhythms of everyday life through a cinematic lens.

Through "Black Girl Anthropology," she seeks to document and celebrate the varied textures of existence, blending visual poetry with rigorous storytelling. Her approach is characterized by an intentional use of space, light, and silence—creating immersive experiences that invite the viewer to see the world anew.
